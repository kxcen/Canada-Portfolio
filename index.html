About Me

Hey There! I'm Kinjelou T. Canada!

But you can call me Kin or Can.

I am a student of Davao del Norte State College and I'm currently immersing myself in the world of technology. Honestly, I've been into technology ever since I started playing computers in local computer shops we call "internetan", with my sights firmly set on becoming a web developer.

My fascination with tech started with a simple curiosity about how the computer works and programming, which quickly turned into a passion for exploring computer parts, learning to fix computer parts, writing clean code, and building things for the browser. For now, I am learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks.

Right now, my main focus is learning as much as I can, experimenting with new tools, and pushing the boundaries of what I can build. When I'm not studying or debugging code, you'll probably find me exploring other areas of technology or working on personal side projects.

I am deeply motivated by the endless possibilities of the tech world, and I’m actively looking for opportunities to learn from experienced developers, collaborate on exciting projects, make programming projects at school, and take my first steps into the industry.
